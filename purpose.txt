# Problem
 - Being an ethical online shopper can be difficult
 - Data on a product's origin may be hard to find
 - Data may not be easy to interpret (when it is available)

# Target Audience
 - Online shoppers concerned with general ethics
 - Online shoppers who want to support a specific cause

# Solutions
 - Deep research tool that leverages mass API and traditional algorithms to gather data
    - Gen. AI is used to present data and determine how (much) and why a product supports a user's cause

# Unique Value Proposition (old)
 - All the necessary data to do deep research on products you buy in one click
    - Evaluate the data at any level of complexity that you are comfortable with to make quick and well-informed decisions that benefit both the world and yourself

# Unique Value Proposition (new)
 - All the necessary data to do deep research on products you buy in in one click
    - Verify and dig deeper into the data at your discretion and comfort level
